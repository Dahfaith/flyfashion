FAQs

How long does delivery take?

Lagos orders: 1–2 business days. Other states: 2–5 business days. We'll send you a tracking number once dispatched.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes! We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn, tagged items. Contact us on WhatsApp to initiate a return.

Do you do bulk / wholesale orders?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders of 10+ items. Message us on WhatsApp or use the form with subject "Custom / Bulk Order".

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer, cash on delivery (Lagos only), Visa/Mastercard, and payment via WhatsApp Pay. Prices available in ₦, $, and £.

Are products authentic and original?

100%. We source directly from verified suppliers and manufacturers. Every product listed is quality-checked before dispatch.
